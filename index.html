Solution

Metageni is a London based provider of expert data analytics using AI to help companies grow online, founded by ex-Google Attribution inventor & pioneer, Gabriel Hughes PhD. Our solutions include a unique approach to machine learning (AI) based data-driven attribution, custom multi-touch attribution (MTA) models, a cross-device attribution system and integrated econometrics with market mix modelling for offline attribution, as well as predictive conversion rate optimisation (CRO) and even bidding. Our results and client case studies prove that custom machine learning adapted to your data and business challenges is your best choice for data-driven marketing, ecommerce efficiency and online sales growth.

How We Work

  • Attribution and Conversion models are most accurate when customised and trained on your data: collaborate with experts to get it right.
  • AI machine learning and predictive analytics has huge potential to leverage your data for growth, but must be deployed with care and expertise.
  • We deliver results within 6 weeks of data access, with 3 to 9 months deployment to your cloud or production stack.
  • Model quality first: transparent custom algorithms, trained on your unique data, adapted to your business needed, for maximum accuracy and impact.
